## Vocal Pitch Ranges

Different voice types have typical pitch ranges:

| Voice Type   | Approx. Range (Hz) |
|--------------|--------------------|
| Bass         | 55–294 Hz          |
| Baritone     | 98–415 Hz          |
| Tenor        | 116–523 Hz         |
| Alto         | 175–784 Hz         |
| Soprano      | 262–1046 Hz        |
